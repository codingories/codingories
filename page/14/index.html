day19_Easy_70_Climbing_Stairs You are climbing a stair case. It takes n steps to reach to the top. Each time you can either climb 1 or 2 steps. In how many distinct ways can you climb to the top? Note: Given n will be a positive integer. Example 1: Input: 2 Output: 2 Explanation: There are two ways to climb to the top.

  1. 1 step + 1 step
  2. 2 steps Example 2: Input: 3 Output: 3 Explanation: There are three ways to climb to the top.
  3. 1 step + 1 step + 1 step
  4. 1 step + 2 steps
  5. 2 steps + 1 step
